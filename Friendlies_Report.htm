Deep Space Report for London Friendlies

Draft report for stats taken during friendlies with Herd 1/2 and Guildford.
Graphs are interactive - hover over data points to see more information.

Overview of Events in Each Game

This section shows the evolution of the game during each point.
Points are color-coded to show which gender ratio has been called, and who calls it (U : Us, T : Them).
Time outs, when they happen, are also shown using vertical dashed lines. These are also color-coded to show which team called the timeout.
The other graph shows the number of possession Deep Space had with each point, and whether that point resulted in DS converting or conceding the point.

Probably useful things to consider:

  • 0 possession points - is this a strong opponent, or is defence lacking
  • High possession numbers, and whether this affects subsequent points
  • Efficient offence would probably be signalled with the team holding 1 possession, and resulting in a conversion. Caveats of course - this doesnâ€™t take into account how many passes it took in the point.

Information by Player

Point Outcomes

The point outcomes are normalised to each player - values represent a percentage of the total points the individual has played.
The "Converted" metric does not take into account Goals/Assists. Total percentage of points that resulted in Deep Space scoring out for each player would be "Goals" + "Assists" + "Converted".
"Converted" metric could possibly be used as a metric to show space-making players, i.e. people being good teammates.
